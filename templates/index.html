About LovingIsSharing

Welcome to LovingIsSharing, where we believe in making shared living spaces harmonious and organized. Our mission is to simplify the management of tasks and responsibilities among flatmates, making cohabitation a breeze.

At LovingIsSharing, we understand the challenges of shared living arrangements. Whether you're in a college dorm, a shared apartment, or a co-living space, coordinating tasks and chores can sometimes be stressful. That's where we come in!

Our user-friendly platform allows you to:

  • Assign tasks and responsibilities to flatmates.
  • Track the progress of tasks and mark them as done.
  • Stay organized with a shared to-do list and calendar.
