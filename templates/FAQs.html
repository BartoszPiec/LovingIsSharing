Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

LovingIsSharing is a web application designed to help flatmates and roommates manage tasks and responsibilities in a shared living space more efficiently. It allows you to create, assign, and track tasks among the members of your household.

To get started, you need to register for an account. After registering and logging in, you can create your household and start inviting your flatmates. Once everyone is linked, you can begin assigning and managing tasks.

To assign a task to one of your flatmates, go to the "Main" page after logging in. Use the "Add Task" form to create a new task, and select the flatmate you want to assign it to from the dropdown list. The assigned task will appear in their task list.
